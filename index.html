📄 Overdraft (OD) / Cash Credit (CC) Facility — What to Expect
Parameter Typical Norm Notes / How Banks Decide Source
When can a new Pvt. Ltd. get OD? After 6–12 months of operation with demonstrable turnover No P&L history — banks watch cash-flow first. Some PSBs insist on 1 audited year. RBI MSME Guidelines
Turnover-Based OD Limit 20%–25% of annual turnover ₹10 Cr turnover → ~₹2–2.5 Cr OD. Actual % depends on sector, collateral, credit score. RBI FAQ
OD Against Collateral (FD / Property) FD: up to 90% · Property: 60–65% Fastest route for a new company with no P&L history. SBI OD
Clean / Unsecured OD Generally not offered to companies < 2 years old Needs 2 years of audited financials + good CIBIL. Fintech NBFCs (Razorpay Capital, Lendingkart) may offer ₹5–50 L unsecured. SIDBI
Interest Rate on OD / CC ~11%–14% p.a. (Repo 6.25% + 2.5–5% spread) Women-founder schemes can trim effective rate by 0.25–0.5%. RBI
CGTMSE-Backed OD Up to ₹2 Cr without collateral No third-party guarantee needed — key advantage for a first-year Pvt. Ltd. CGTMSE Portal
Tip: Bring your partnership firm's last 2–3 years of bank statements + ITR to the RM meeting — banks use it as a proxy for business vintage to fast-track OD approval.

  • Loans ₹10 L – ₹1 Cr for women entrepreneurs; composite term loan + working capital

  • Women hold >50% ownership → 0.05% interest concession on loans >₹2 L
  • Covers trade, small industries, professional services

  • Shishu ≤₹50K · Kishor ₹50K–₹5L · Tarun ₹5L–₹10L · no collateral
  • Women-led businesses get priority disbursement
  • udyamimitra.in

  • Concessional rates + CGTMSE cover for women-owned MSMEs
  • Zero processing fees on select schemes
  • BoB MSME

SIDBI Mahila Udyam Nidhi SIDBI

  • sidbi.in

✍ Recommended Next Steps
  1. Apply for DPIIT recognition at startupindia.gov.in — unlocks 0-balance accounts + fee waivers at ICICI, HDFC, Axis, Kotak.
  2. Shortlist ICICI + SBI and book RM meetings with: CoI, MOA/AOA, PAN, GST certificate, last 2–3 yrs partnership firm statements + ITR, Director ID proofs.
  3. Ask the RM about: women-founder concession · OD eligibility on past firm turnover · CGTMSE coverage · processing fee waivers.
  4. Before signing, Google "[Bank] Current Account Schedule of Charges 2026 PDF" for the legally binding fee list.
  5. Check Stand-Up India eligibility at standupmitra.in — every scheduled bank must have a dedicated nodal officer.
